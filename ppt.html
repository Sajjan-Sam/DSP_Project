  • LSTM: No natural quantile output
  • Prophet/SARIMA: Assume Gaussian errors (false!)

  • Learn œÑ-quantile of errors
  • Adjust intervals based on inputs

Conformal Prediction Calibration

  • Finite-sample coverage guarantee
  • Model-agnostic adjustment
